接收患者的基本信息（年龄、BMI、诊断、用药、过敏、饮食偏好），
结合循证医学证据，生成一份针对该患者的个性化饮食建议，
包含每日热量目标、营养素比例、食物推荐与禁忌，
并附上支持该建议的医学文献引用。

输出格式：表格（每日饮食结构）+ 文字说明

Take the patient's basic information (age, BMI, diagnosis, current medications, allergies, and dietary preferences) and generate a personalized, evidence-based dietary plan. The plan should include:

Daily caloric target
Recommended macronutrient ratios (carbohydrates, protein, fat)
Specific foods to include and foods to avoid
Supporting citations from peer-reviewed medical literature
Output Format: A structured table showing the daily meal framework, followed by a written summary explaining the key dietary recommendations and their clinical rationale.
