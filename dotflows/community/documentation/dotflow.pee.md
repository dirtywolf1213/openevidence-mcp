Add a full adult Physical Exam  to the note, in document form, using the document template below. Please put it in its own Section header labeled "Physical Exam," in bold font. Place this section, Physical Exam, right after vital signs. If vitals are unavailable, place this section right after the Review of Systems section.  Make all items prior to the colons in the document in Bold Font:   

General: NAD, Alert, well-appearing. Vital signs noted

Eyes: Lids/lashes/orbit unremarkable. Conjunctiva not injected and without discharge

Sclera are anicteric and not injected. Pupils are equal, round, and reactive to light
Extraocular muscles are intact

HENT: EAC non-tender, no lesions. TM's clear, positive COL B/L. 
No nasal discharge  
No pharyngeal erythema, edema, or exudate

Neck: supple, no masses, bruits, or LAD. No thyromegaly or nodules appreciated

Resp: No deformities. Clear to auscultation. Bilaterally, no wheeze, cough, or rales. Effort
normal, no stridor

CVS: S1S2 RRR No murmurs, rubs, or gallops. Pulses symmetrical and regular.

Abdom: Soft, non-distended, no tenderness to palpation, no HSM appreciated

GU: No CVA tenderness, no tenderness noted over the bladder

MSS: No tenderness, deformity, or edema. No clubbing or cyanosis noted. Full ROM

NS: Alert, oriented to person, place, and time. 
Gait WNL CN 2-12 intact 
Strength 5/5 in all 4 extremities 
Sensation intact 
DTR's symmetrical 2+ in all 4 extremities 
Skin of the hindfoot and forefoot intact B/L without lesions or erosions noted

Skin: No new suspicious lesions or rashes noted. No jaundice, pallor, or plethora noted
Nails show no clubbing. No cyanosis noted

Lymph: No lymphadenopathy noted or appreciated

Psych: Affect appropriate, speech normal. AAO x 3, good thought content and judgment,
PHQ-2 negative, mood stable, denies suicidal ideations. 

Describe normal findings using the wording in the template above. If abnormal findings are discussed or found on exam, replace the normal findings in this document with the abnormal findings discussed or described.
