Add a full adult systemic review of systems to the note, in a document form using the document template below, in its own Section header labeled "Review of Systems," in bold font. Place this section, Review of Systems, before the objective data section. Make all items prior to the colons in the document in Bold Font:   

General: Constitutional: no fever, no chills, no malaise, no fatigue, no night sweats, no
significant weight loss, no significant weight gain, no exercise intolerance

Eyes: no dry eyes, no irritation, no vision change, no diplopia, no blurred vision, no
pain in or around the eyes

HENT: Ears: no difficulty hearing, no ear pain, no feeling of fullness, no tinnitus 
Nose: no congestion, no frequent nosebleeds, no sinus complaints, normal sense of smell.
Mouth/Throat: no sore throat, no post-nasal drip, no bleeding gums, no lip sores, no dry
mouth, no mouth sores, no teeth problems, no thrush

Resp: Respiratory: no cough, no congestion, no sputum, no hemoptysis, no wheezing, no
shortness of breath, no DOE

CVS: Cardiovascular: no chest pain, no palpitations, no shortness of breath, no dyspnea on
exertion, no orthopnea, no arm pain on exertion, no claudication, no edema

GI: Gastrointestinal: no abdominal pain, no change in stool, no blood in stool, no
constipation, no diarrhea, no bloating, normal appetite, no dysphagia, no heartburn, no
nausea, no vomiting

GU: Genitourinary: no dysuria, no frequency, no hesitancy, no urgency, no nocturia, no
hematuria, no incontinence, no discharge

MSS: Musculoskeletal: no muscle aches, no muscle weakness, no arthralgias/joint pain, no
back pain, no neck pain

NS: Neurologic: no loss of consciousness, no weakness, no tremors, no seizures, no
dizziness, no headaches, no memory lapses or loss, no numbness

Skin: no rash, no yellow skin or eyes. Hair: no hair loss. Nails: normal nails, no
longitudinal ridges, no pitting, no discoloration

Hemo: No abnormal bleeding or bruising

Endoc: No heat or cold intolerance, no abnormal loss of hair. No polydipsia

Psych: Denies anxiety or depressive symptoms.

Use the above documentation if no abnormalities are found.  If abnormalities are noted, replace the normal document wording with the abnormal descriptions.
