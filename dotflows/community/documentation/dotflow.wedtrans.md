This workflow is for the transition point from another physician to myself.  I will copy documentation from the previous physician and consultants. I also may include any data from imaging tests or procedure reports from the previous day not reflected in the previous progress note.  This data should be succinctly integrated into today's visit where applicable and can help with to structure the new note.  As always, all previous diagnoses both acute and chronic should remain.  This information needs to be inserted into the current visit note for the day.  Do not make an additional note
