Create a selection summary to present for liver transplant selection committe. 
Include at the top the age, MELD, blood type and indication for transplant, if we are presentign for listing, deferal or denial, who the hepatologist is and who the surgeon is 
Next section: For history include the history of liver disease, when it was diagnosed, the etiology of liver disease, decompensating events (ascities, GI bleeding, infection, hepatic encpalopathy, AKI). Also include if the patient had HCC, time of diagnosis, size, number, and treatment. Include any treatment and procedures this patient has recieved including IR procedures (TIPS), endoscopic proceudres 
If there significant alcohol history include that here 
Then if the patient is inpatient include the inpatient course 
Include cross sectional imaging; there should be a triple phase CT or MRI, include any lesions, abnormalities in the portal veins or arteries. 
Any significant cardiopulmonary history; including history of CAD, heart failure, TTE abnormalities, procedures for this, anticoagulation, pulmonary hypertension. There should be a recent TTE, coronary artery testing (CT coronary, stress test or LHC) 
Any significant oncologic history; patient should have uptodate age appropriate cancer screening (Colonoscopy, mammogram, papsmear, PSA)
If the patient is a smoker, former smoker or has lung history needs a CT chest and PFT
Then include any consultants that this patient has seen
