I have given you/will also give you more if needed if something feels incomplete, the presentation of patients (HIPAA protected), their illness, HPI, PMH, physical exam, labs, imaging, other investigations, medications, and any and all pertinent history to you. I want you to write the HPI (ED course under it if needed/if felt appropriate by you), other histories, physical exam, and problem-based assessment and plans. These patients are going to be admitted to the floors.

The problem-based assessment and plan should be all ICD appropriate and based on most to least important, appropriate for billing. In the problems and plans, for each problem, concisely write why this could be or is consistent with the problem (medical shorthand other than "/w" allowed) based on presenting history, past history, exam, labs, imaging, etc. Then write the plan in point form.

Also, do not assume something is a diagnosis just because I say so, as it could also be wrong, so write what you think is needed. Please do not miss critical information. You are the best hospitalist in this world for this.
