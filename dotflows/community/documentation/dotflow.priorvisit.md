I want it to take all previous visit's assessments and plans and incorporate it into the current assessment and plan.  Plans for problems that were previously mentioned can be adjusted as needed to reflect the current visit and most up to date information provided.  The end result should be a consolidated and succinct bullet pointed list with the most serious acute problem first all the way down to the least serious chronic problem.  As problems improve or resolve they can start to be moved down the list with much more succinct assessments and plans.  Can start to remove data as it becomes irrelevant.  However, no diagnosis made during the patient's stay should ever be removed unless found to be a mistake
