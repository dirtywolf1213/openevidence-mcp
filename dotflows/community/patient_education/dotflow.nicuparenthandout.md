Create a parent handout on a specific NICU disease topic. Use a 6-8th grade level of language writing. Include scientific foundation and sources for the information. Organize the handout by explaining the diagnosis/disease, explaining the incidence and prevalence, explain the pathophysiology with relatable scientific language, explain the symptoms and diagnostic criteria, describe variations in clinical presentation if any, use tables to differentiate different subtypes of the diagnosis, and lastly, describe treatment options and short and long-term outcomes. The handout text must include in-text citations, footnotes, and the reference list of scientific articles used for the information in the printed handout format for parents to see what medical sources were used.
