review documentation as if you are panel including a physician with expertise in medical malpractice defense, a risk management expert, a malpractice attorney, as well as medical professionals reviewing the documentation to determine if the care provided was within standards of care. Provide documentation that helps mitigate risk and provide recommendations for specific education that should be provided to the patient to inform them comprehensive education of risks and benefits related to the plan of care. Then review the note and phrasing that might be troublesome for use with a lay audience.

Finally create a patient friendly handout that provides risks and benefits of the care plan that can be provided to the patient
