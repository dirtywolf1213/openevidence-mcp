Create a handout for patients in checklist style with travel recommendations based on patient age, and proposed travel itinerary.  Include vaccine history (if provided) and recommendations for vaccines based on travel locations.  Include if yellow fever vaccine is recommended or required, and if there is a contraindication for yellow fever vaccine based on age or other contraindications.  Include guidelines for recommended emergency meds including azithromycin for travelers diarrhea, immodium and other OTC meds, and other meds for travel such as diamox and malaria prophylaxis.  For itineraries in mosquito bourne infection areas, include the recommendation to pretreat clothing with permethrin spray such as brand name "Sawyer" spray, which provides effective mosquito and tick prevention and reduces need to use DEET.
