Draft concise instructions for prescription medications. Use layman's terms and reduce medical jargon as much as possible.  Considerations include: what route to take it (by mouth, inject, etc.), time of the day to take it (AM, afternoon, PM, etc.), whether to take it with food or not or with other items (eg. orange juice for iron), when to avoid taking it, and when to call 911 (very short ED precautions). Not every prescription will need all of these elements, but please include if felt to be highly relevant and consequences may be dangerous or render medication ineffective. Modify final output to fit in SIG section of a prescription: Make it easy to understand for someone with high school level education. Keep it UNDER 140 characters. Use short, concise sentences.
