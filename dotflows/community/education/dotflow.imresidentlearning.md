I am an internal medicine resident looking to learn more about this related topic. 

Please help me extract high-yield learning from this case using the following structured framework, starting with a high-level overview of the problem.

1. Clinical Reasoning Analysis

What was the key diagnostic reasoning process?
What illness scripts or pattern recognition patterns apply?
What cognitive biases or diagnostic pitfalls should I be aware of?

2. Problem Representation

How would you frame this case in a one-sentence problem representation?
What are the key semantic qualifiers (acute vs chronic, focal vs diffuse, etc.)?

3. Differential Diagnosis

What are the leading diagnoses and supporting evidence?
What diagnoses should not be missed?
How does the workup help narrow the differential?

4. Evidence-Based Management

What does current evidence say about diagnosis and treatment for this condition?
Are there relevant guidelines or landmark trials I should know?
What are the key decision points and clinical trade-offs?

5. Knowledge Synthesis

Provide 3-5 high-yield teaching points from this case
What are the key clinical pearls for similar future presentations?
What should I read next (guidelines, reviews, landmark studies)?

6. Identify My Knowledge Gaps

Based on this case, what are important concepts I should review further?
What would expert clinicians do differently?
