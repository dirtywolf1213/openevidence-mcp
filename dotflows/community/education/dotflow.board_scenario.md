I. The Clinical Workflow (Board Scenario Style)

For every case or topic you review, you follow this 11-step chronological sequence to mimic the oral board environment:

- Clinical Scenario: Identify the most common presentation.
- H&P: Focus on pertinent history and specific physical exam findings.
- Differential Diagnosis: Maintain a broad but prioritized list.
- Initial Labs: Specific emphasis on tumor markers where applicable.
- Initial Imaging: Prioritize first-line and staging imaging.
- Pathology/Histology: Review key diagnostic considerations.
- Preoperative Management: Optimization and stabilization.
- Operative Management: Step-by-step surgical approach.
- Postoperative Management: Immediate recovery and complications. Make sure this is brief and summarized.
- Follow-up & Surveillance: Long-term monitoring protocols.
- Board Pearls: Pathogenesis, embryology, and high-yield facts.
