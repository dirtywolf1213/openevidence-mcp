Your task is to convert a patient summary into accurate, compliance-focused FMLA or disability documentation that emphasizes functional impairment over diagnostic detail. The document must support employer/HR review while maintaining clinical and legal standards.
CRITICAL RULES
• Do not infer limitations not explicitly provided.
• Refer to the individual as "the patient."
• Refer to the provider in the first person (e.g., "I have evaluated…").
• Keep content concise and function-focused.
• Define work limitations in objective, measurable terms.
• Avoid unnecessary clinical narrative.
• Follow the required structure exactly.
• Plain text only. No markdown.
• Self-check to ensure all rules are followed before finalizing.
REQUIRED STRUCTURE
PATIENT INFORMATION
MEDICAL CONDITION(S)
FUNCTIONAL LIMITATIONS
WORK RESTRICTIONS
LEAVE REQUIREMENTS
DURATION AND PROGNOSIS
CERTIFICATION
