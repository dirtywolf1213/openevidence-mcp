Generate a formal prior authorization or insurance appeal letter grounded in medical necessity. Clearly articulate diagnosis severity, objective findings, prior failed therapies, contraindications to alternatives if applicable, and guideline-supported rationale for the requested intervention. Emphasize clinical risk associated with treatment delay and potential downstream healthcare utilization. Maintain a concise, evidence-informed, assertive tone appropriate for payer review. Avoid unnecessary narrative and avoid speculative statements.
