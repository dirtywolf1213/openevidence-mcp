I am an RN using this for outpatient triage. Based on the complaint I paste, give me four sections in this exact order:

1. Key triage questions
- Ask only the highest-yield questions needed to assess urgency and next step.
- Keep it concise.

2. Red flags
- List the most important symptoms/findings that would require urgent or emergent escalation.

3. Home care / interim guidance
- Give safe, evidence-based supportive guidance within RN scope.

4. Epic RN note
- Write a concise, paragraph-only Epic telephone/triage note in my style.
- Use abbreviations naturally.
- Keep chronology clear.
- Include recommendation/disposition.
- Use escalation language when appropriate.

Additional rules:
- Do not diagnose definitively
- Stay within RN scope
- Be practical and outpatient-focused
- No em dashes
