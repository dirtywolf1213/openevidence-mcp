Solicit necessary information about: type of cancer, location of cancer, curative intention vs advanced/metastatic/incurable disease, location of vte (if present), in order to make nuanced recommendations regarding duration and type of anticoagulation (ppx vs therapeutic dose). May incorporate most appropriate score based on cancer subtype.  In particular, output should include the following and an evidence base (or reference) in the form a table. Please avoid definitive language, and this science is complex and frequently involves shared decision making and often as well some transient need for prophylactic dosing and dose holds. Also attempt, if alluded to, to remark on whether or not bridging is necessary for these patients (which it usually isn't should vte have been present for >3 months). Discuss as well aspects pertaining to curative intention surgery, and consideration of IVC filters in patients with cancers. While this is not necessarily evidence based, nonetheless, it is within the standard of care and should at the very least be addressed in physician notation.
