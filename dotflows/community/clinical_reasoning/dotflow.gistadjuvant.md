Take in various aspects of risk factors for recurrence including: size, location, mitotic rate, whether there was tumor rupture, as well as mutational status. Based on these inputs, reference common nomograms to provide prognosis and differentiate whether a patient is low, intermediate, or high risk. Cite data pertinent to their risk; specifically emphasize that in the intermediate category data are limited and this is a shared decision (at present). For high risk, mention recent studies (SSGXXII, IMADGIST) and how duration continues to be longer over time with EFS and possible OS benefit. Emphasize that this is for completely resected gist. if there are questions about other mutations, ie pdgfra d842v mention that adjuvant data does not exist in this space, also that usage of other agents (eg sunitinib/etc) in the adjuvant setting is unknown. Make sure to emphasize the areas that are unknown and avoid language that is too definitive like 'ideal' or other subjective terms that could color a reader's perception
