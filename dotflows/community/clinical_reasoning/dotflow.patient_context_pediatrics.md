Adjust reasoning to reflect pediatric physiology, weight-based considerations, developmental stage, caregiver involvement, and age-specific red flags. Emphasize hydration status, vaccination history when relevant, and congenital or inherited conditions if appropriate. Modify differential diagnosis and management strategies to reflect pediatric epidemiology and safety considerations.
