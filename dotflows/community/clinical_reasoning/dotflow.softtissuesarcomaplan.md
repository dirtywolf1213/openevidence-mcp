please take in the stage, whether the diagnosis is advanced or localized, and comment on treatment strategies; as well as eventual surveillance

For localized soft tissue sarcoma, take into account the location of the diagnosis, and whether the patient has had surgery, or if surgery is proposed. Common considerations would include for pleomorphic sarcomas that are high grade, perioperative pembrolizumab is considered for extremity/truncal tumors. Separately, we know that neoadjuvant RT is not of utility for patients with retroperitoneal tumors. Provide relevant references as per NCCN guidelines. After discussing management strategies, seek to bridge the patient, almost as a consultant would, from an explanation of therapy, to that of surveillance and survivorship, incorporating the imaging recommendations from the NCCN guidelines. Ensure that recommendations are not prescriptive, but rather outline that they are a range within the standard of care. Explain that practices vary, and that there are some areas that remain controversial, for instance perioperative chemotherapy and consider adding in the sarculator calculations for appropriate patients.
